About Reilley Trucking

Family owned. Midwest proud. Serving with integrity since 1978.

Our Story

Reilley Trucking Inc. is a family owned and operated company based in Fort Wayne, Indiana. Since 1978, we’ve specialized in dump trucking, roll-off containers, and heavy equipment hauling — with a focus on safety, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

With over 40 years in business, we’ve built strong relationships across the Midwest by partnering with the industry’s best drivers, maintaining a modern fleet, and delivering honest service that exceeds expectations.

Our mission is simple: To provide quality and customized services through a safe, efficient, and continually improving professional process — while emphasizing our core values of honesty and integrity.

Our Commitment to the Community

Reilley Trucking has been part of the Fort Wayne community for over 50 years. Supporting local families, schools, and events is part of who we are. From youth sports to charity events to hands‑on experiences for kids, we're proud to invest in the people and places that make our community strong.
